Reminders

A nudge for anything you pay regularly or need to remember — a bill, a subscription, anything. This only shows while you have the app open (no background phone notifications without a server), but it'll greet you here, on Insights, and on the bell icon.

Leave at 0 to only be reminded on the due date. Set to e.g. 2 to get a daily nudge starting 2 days before, every day until it's due (only while the app is open).

Off — only works while this tab is open.

Track money you're saving for something — a trip, a gadget, an emergency fund. Adding a contribution here just updates your progress; it doesn't create a transaction in your ledger, since this money is still yours, just set aside.
